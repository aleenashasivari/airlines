Passenger Rules

Baggage Rules

  • Maximum carry-on size: 22"x14"x9".
  • Checked luggage weight limit: 50 lbs.

What You Can Carry

  • Personal items such as books, jackets, and electronics.
  • Liquids under 100ml in clear, resealable bags.

If Your Bag is Lost

If your luggage is lost, please report it to our customer service desk immediately. We will assist you with tracking and compensation.
